Crustghetti

Spaghetti with a thin layer of crust.

The ancient people found this recipe deep within the catacombs. It is feared by many.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Other

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta in hot water with salt
  2. Drain water from recipient. STORE WATER AWAY FOR LATER!
  3. Pour sauce on spaghetti
  4. Cleanse the body with spaghetti water
  5. Add crust
  6. Consume.
